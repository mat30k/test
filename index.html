Latest Project

FBX

FBX is a project that aims to streamline the process of data transfer between ERP (Enterprise Resource Planning) and Warehouse Management Systems. Acting as a powerful middleware, the solution seamlessly connects these two essential systems, offering a suite of robust and reliable functionalities. The platform is easily adaptable to meet the unique needs of individual businesses, ensuring an efficient and hassle-free flow of information. With FBX, businesses can enjoy a fast, reliable, and cost-effective solution for their ERP-WMS integration needs.

Technologies used:

  • TypeScript
  • Node.js
  • MariaDB
  • PHP MyAdmin
  • AWS
